Invoicing Software

The invoicing software developed consists in a tool that helps kromatronics c.a. to generate a bill for products or services they have provided to clients. Each invoice that is created in the web application consists of a list with the services or products with their corresponding prices, and the total amount of money the client have to pay to generete the envoice.

The software developed is used mostly to perform accounting and financial tasks as well as the creation of envoices. The envoicing software is based on PHP, MySQL, JavaScript & frameworks, HTML5 and CSS3.
